# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We want this project to be a welcoming space for people improving Philippine mapping, data tooling, and local-first civic software.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Assume good intent, but accept corrections.
- Keep critique focused on the work.
- Make space for beginners and domain experts alike.
- Be careful with claims about official geographic data.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, threats, or personal attacks.
- Demeaning comments about identity, background, or experience level.
- Publishing private information without consent.
- Repeatedly derailing technical or data-quality discussions.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove content, reject contributions, or restrict participation when needed to keep the project healthy.
